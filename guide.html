Economy Guide

Coralshire introduces a highly realistic and dynamic economy system designed to enhance the roleplaying experience, making it feel immersive and grounded in a believable, functional world. In this system, your in-game income is directly tied to the job you take on, the time and effort you invest, and the skills you develop throughout your journey. The economy isn't just a backdrop but a core part of the experience, giving players a true sense of accomplishment as they progress.

In Coralshire, players begin their economic journey by taking on various entry-level jobs that serve as stepping stones to more advanced and lucrative opportunities. The system starts simple, allowing newcomers to earn money without feeling overwhelmed. These early tasks may include activities such as farming, collecting resources, or providing basic services like delivering goods. Players can quickly start building up their resources, gaining small amounts of currency while learning the basics of the economy. However, earning becomes more complex as you progress. You can choose from a wide variety of professions, each with its own set of skills, challenges, and rewards. For example, you could become a blacksmith crafting weapons and tools, a merchant buying and selling goods, or even a skilled tradesperson like a carpenter, tailor, or miner. The more you specialize and hone your abilities, the greater your potential earnings, but it takes time and effort to reach higher-paying jobs.

Vehicle Keybinds Guide

Here’s a breakdown of the essential keybinds for operating your car in Coralshire:
W – Accelerate
A – Turn Left
S – Brake
D – Turn Right

For more precise control and safety while driving:
P – Parking Brake
H – Horn
Z – Left Indicator
X – Hazards
C – Right Indicator
L – Headlights

Emergency vehicles come with additional controls:
R – Wail Siren
T – Yelp Siren
G – Priority Siren
J – Activate 999 Mode/Change Lighting Mode
K – Activate Red/Blue Lights
