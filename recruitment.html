Careers in Law Enforcement

We are looking for dedicated individuals to serve and protect our city. Join our team today!

Available Positions

  • Patrol Officer – Ensure public safety and respond to emergencies.
  • Detective – Investigate criminal cases and gather evidence.
  • Dispatch Operator – Handle emergency calls and coordinate responses.
  • Community Liaison Officer – Work closely with residents to improve community relations.

Minimum Requirements

  • Must be at least 21 years old
  • Valid driver's license
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Clean criminal record
