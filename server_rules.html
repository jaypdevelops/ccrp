Server Rules

Introduction

Coral County Roleplay is an extremely strict, but sometimes casual, roleplay game. Meaning that we have very high standards for member conduct. We have created a rules list so members know what they can, and can't, do in a server.

BASIC RULES

1. Innapropriate language is allowed. However, swearing too frequently may result in moderation.

2. Use of racial, homophobic, sexist or any other discriminative slurs are NOT tolerated at all. Use of any will get you immediately banned, without a possibility to appeal.

3. Disrespect towards members will not be tolerated and will result in immediate moderation.

4. Disrespect towards community staff is also not tolerated and will also result in immediate moderation.

ROLEPLAY RULES

5. Any abuse of 911 call systems is not tolerated and will result in moderation.

6. Fail Roleplay (FRP) is not tolerated and will result in immediate moderation.

7. Random Deathmatch (RDM) is not tolerated and will result in immediate heavy moderation.

8. GTA Driving is not tolerated. Pleade attempt to drive as realistically as possible.
