Chat Commands

/e me: Displays text above your head correlating to an action.

/e ooc: Shows a tag above your head displaying an out of roleplay action or information.

/e youfind: Displays text, showing the officer what they find when they search you.

/e hands: Displays text, showing the officer that your hands are up.

/lock (changing to /e lock) Locks your vehicle.

/unlock (changing to /e unlock) Unlocks your vehicle.

Vehicle Keybinds


Z: Activates your left indicator.

X: Activates your hazards.

C: Activates your right indicator.

F/H: Toggles your horn. The keybind depends on which type of vehicle you have. (Emergency = F, Civilian = H)

L: Toggles Headlights.

P: Parking Brake.

B: Toggles Seatbelt.


Emergency Vehicle Keybinds


J: Toggles ELS/999 Mode.

R: Wail Siren.

T: Yelp Siren.

G: Priority Siren.

H: Hi-Low Siren.

F (while 999 mode active): Activates Bullhorn.

K: Activates Rear Red Lights.

Additional


ELS Controller: If you are driving an emergency vehicle and have locked it, when getting into the drivers seat, you are still able to activate/deactivate lights, sirens and other ELS related items.
