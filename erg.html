About Us

The Emergency Response Group (ERG) is a charity-based organization that supports emergency services by responding to incidents where immediate police, fire, or ambulance intervention isn't required. We provide medical aid, secure scenes, and assist the public in a safe and controlled manner.

What We Do

  • Medical response before ambulances arrive
  • Scene safety at RTCs
  • Policing for events where regular police is not available
  • Support to emergency services in low-risk situations

We operate a range of vehicles including ambulances, fire vehicles, and air units.
